### The One Asset to Build Before Anything Else

**Momentum vs. Streak graphic.**

This is the most shareable, most Canon-aligned piece of content in the launch. Build it during pre-launch, not during launch week. Use it on Day 1.

Two lines. No words needed beyond labels:

```
Streak model:    ✓ ✓ ✓ ✗ → ZERO

Momentum model:  ✓ ✓ ✓ ✗ → continues
```

Keep it clean. This graphic explains Nelson's core insight faster than any paragraph.

### Messaging Alignment

Not "download the app." Scarcity is real — use it.

## LAUNCH WEEK TIMELINE

### DAY 1 — TUESDAY (LAUNCH DAY)

---

**Morning: Email Blast**

Send to: alpha users + waitlist

Subject options:
- "Nelson is live — Founding Members open"
- "After months of building… Nelson is live"

Structure:
1. Short founder note (3–4 sentences max)
2. The restart cycle insight (2–3 sentences)
3. App Store link
4. Founding Member explanation — what it is, what it costs, why it's limited
5. Direct invitation to join early

Keep it short. The goal is a click, not a read.

---

**Morning: Alpha User Personal Note (send separately, same morning)**

Do not fold this into the general blast. These people tested the product. They deserve a direct message.

Tone: personal, not broadcast.

Structure:
- Thank them for being part of the alpha
- Tell them the app is live today
- Tell them Founding Member spots are open
- Ask them to share feedback, invite a friend, leave a review

Alpha users are your first evangelists. Treat them like it from Day 1.

---

**Morning: LinkedIn Post**

Format: founder story launch post

Structure:
1. The restart cycle (hook — personal, specific)
2. The insight about streaks (the false belief)
3. Building Nelson (brief)
4. Launch announcement
5. Founding Member availability + link

Tone: adult-to-adult. No hype. No exclamation marks.

---

**Morning: TikTok / Instagram Reel**

Keep it under 45 seconds.

Script structure:
- Miss one day
- Streak resets
- People quit
- That's the restart cycle
- Nelson measures momentum instead
- App just launched — link in bio

Use the momentum vs. streak graphic as a visual anchor if the format allows.

---

**Evening: Monitor and Respond**

Check:
- Downloads
- Founding Member purchase conversions
- Onboarding drop-off points

Respond to:
- Every comment
- Every DM
- Every email reply

Early interaction matters more than reach. One genuine conversation with a new user is worth more than 1,000 impressions today.

---

### DAY 2 — WEDNESDAY

**Goal: reinforce launch, don't repeat it**

Post type: educational content

Topic: Why streaks break people

Explain the restart cycle visually. Show the pattern. Let the insight land without selling.

Platforms: LinkedIn + Instagram carousel + TikTok short explanation

CTA: "Nelson launched yesterday. Link in bio."

---

### DAY 3 — THURSDAY

**Goal: credibility**

Share early user feedback.

Options:
- Direct quotes from alpha users (ask permission)
- Screenshots of the momentum graph (with permission)
- Early observations about what people are noticing

Authenticity over polish. A real quote from a real user beats any marketing copy.

---

### DAY 4 — FRIDAY

**Goal: remind people about Founding Member scarcity**

Post theme: "Why we limited Founding Members to 50."

Content:
- Early supporters help shape the product
- Building in public — these users get direct input
- The price locks for life as long as the subscription stays active
- This is not a marketing gimmick — there are genuinely only 50 spots

Make the offer feel real. Because it is.

---

### DAY 4 OR 5 — AMPLIFIER MOMENT (300K IG Activation)

**Do not do this on Day 1.**

Wait until:
- The product has been live for 3–4 days
- Onboarding issues have been identified and fixed
- Early testimonials exist
- Some Founding Member slots are already filled

That audience should see proof, not just a launch announcement.

Brief the friend on the messaging before they post:
- The restart cycle insight
- What Nelson actually does
- That Founding Member spots are limited and filling

One post. One moment. Time it right.

---

### DAY 5 — SATURDAY

**Optional: founder reflection**

Topic: "What I learned building Nelson."

Keep it human. No selling. This is a credibility and connection post, not a conversion post.

Only post if you have something genuine to say. Skip it if you're running on empty from the week.

---

### DAYS 6–7 — SUNDAY / MONDAY

Only post if something real happened:
- A milestone (first 25 Founding Members, first 50)
- A piece of user feedback worth sharing
- An unexpected insight from launch week

Otherwise let the week breathe. You've done the work.

## LAUNCH WEEK METRICS

**Track:**
- Downloads

- Week-1 retention (did people check in after Day 1?)
- Quality of user feedback

- Follower counts
- Likes
- Impressions

Those are noise. 50 paying users is signal.
